## 2. TOP 5 MONEY KEYWORDS

These are the keywords most likely to generate actual paying leads, ranked by lead quality and feasibility:

### #1: "cabinet refinishing Crestview FL"
- **Why this is money:** High-ticket service ($500-$3,000+ per job), very few competitors specifically targeting this keyword at handyman price points. Dedicated cabinet shops charge 2-3x more. Andy already does this work.
- **Action:** Build a dedicated landing page with before/after photos, pricing guidance, and a quote request form.
- **Expected difficulty:** Easy to rank. 3-6 months to page 1.

### #2: "handyman Crestview FL"
- **Why this is money:** Highest volume search term in the category. Every lead is someone actively looking for exactly what Andy offers.
- **Action:** Optimized homepage + Google Business Profile. Service area pages.
- **Expected difficulty:** Medium. Ace Handyman and directory sites dominate, but a well-optimized local site with reviews can crack top 5.

### #3: "painting contractor Crestview FL"
- **Why this is money:** Second highest volume. Painting jobs are consistent revenue. Most competitors in this space are either large operations (CertaPro, BDS Construction) or tiny with no web presence.
- **Action:** Dedicated painting page with interior/exterior sections, photo gallery.
- **Expected difficulty:** Medium. Several established players, but few handyman-priced painters have real websites.

### #4: "roof leak repair Crestview FL"
- **Why this is money:** Urgent need = fastest conversion. People searching this need help NOW. Full roofing companies target "roof replacement" and "roofing contractor" but often ignore small leak repairs.
- **Action:** Dedicated page targeting minor/emergency roof repairs. Blog post: "Is It a Small Leak or Do You Need a New Roof?"
- **Expected difficulty:** Medium-low. Roofing companies own "roof repair" broadly, but "roof leak repair" at handyman scale is underserved.

### #5: "handyman near Eglin AFB" / "home repairs near Eglin AFB"
- **Why this is money:** Military families are ideal customers: steady income, frequent moves (move-in/move-out repairs), and they talk to each other (word of mouth on base). Nobody in the market is targeting this audience specifically.
- **Action:** Dedicated landing page or blog post targeting military families. Mention proximity to Eglin, Duke Field, Hurlburt Field.
- **Expected difficulty:** Very easy to rank. Zero competition for these exact terms.

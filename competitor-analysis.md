## 3. Top 3 Keyword Opportunities

### Opportunity 1: "cabinet refinishing Crestview FL"
- **Why:** Very few handymen in the area specifically claim this service. Dedicated cabinet shops (Creative Cabinets, Cabinets of Distinction) charge premium prices. Andy already does this work. A single well-optimized landing page could own this keyword.
- **Competition:** Low for handyman-priced cabinet refinishing. High-end cabinet shops dominate but target different price points.

### Opportunity 2: "handyman near Eglin AFB" / "home repairs near Eglin AFB"
- **Why:** Large military population constantly rotating in/out. They need quick, affordable repairs for move-in/move-out. Nobody is specifically targeting military-adjacent keywords.
- **Competition:** Very low. No competitor specifically targets Eglin/military audience.

### Opportunity 3: "roof leak repair Crestview FL" (handyman-level)
- **Why:** Dedicated roofers (Timberman, Quality Roofing, Guy Brothers) dominate for full roof replacement, but there is a gap for small leak repairs that don't need a full roofing contractor. Andy can capture the "minor roof repair" segment.
- **Competition:** Medium. Roofers own the broad terms but don't target small jobs specifically.

## 5. The #1 Thing We Can Do That NONE of Them Are Doing

### Video Testimonials + Project Walkthroughs

**Not a single competitor in this market has video content on their website.**

The opportunity:
- Short (30-60 second) phone-recorded videos of Andy walking through a completed project
- Quick customer testimonial clips ("Andy just finished our kitchen cabinets, here's what they look like")
- Before/during/after video stories for major jobs (roof repair, cabinet refinishing, painting)

**Why this wins:**
1. **Trust multiplier:** In a market where most competitors have zero web presence, video content is 10x more persuasive than stock photos
2. **SEO advantage:** Google heavily favors pages with embedded video. YouTube videos rank in Google search results
3. **Local authenticity:** Andy's 27 years of experience and personal approach are his biggest differentiator -- video conveys this better than any text
4. **Unreplicable:** Franchise competitors (Ace) can't match the personal, local feel. Solo operators (CBond, PMB) don't have the technical capability
5. **Social proof pipeline:** Same videos work on Facebook, YouTube, Google Business Profile, and the website

Combined with a clean, mobile-first website that has service-specific pages, a quote request form, and those 18 Yelp photos migrated over, Affordable Home Repairs would go from invisible online to the most credible handyman in Crestview overnight.

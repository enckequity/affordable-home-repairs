# Affordable Home Repairs LLC — Brand Guide

## Colors

| Token            | Hex       | Role                                    |
| ---------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Primary          | `#1B4332` | Deep forest green — trust, craftsmanship, grounded |
| Primary Light    | `#2D6A4F` | Hover / lighter green accents           |
| Primary Dark     | `#0B2318` | Headings, high-contrast text            |
| Secondary        | `#D4A373` | Warm tan — Southern warmth, wood tones  |
| Secondary Light  | `#E6C9A8` | Subtle backgrounds, borders             |
| Accent           | `#E76F51` | Burnt orange — energy, CTA buttons      |
| Accent Dark      | `#C4563B` | Hover state for accent CTAs             |
| Background       | `#FAFAF8` | Warm cream — inviting, clean            |
| Background Alt   | `#F0EDE8` | Alternating section backgrounds         |
| Text             | `#1A1A1A` | Body copy                               |
| Text Light       | `#4A4A4A` | Secondary body copy                     |
| Text Muted       | `#6B7280` | Captions, metadata                      |

**Rationale:** The palette avoids the typical contractor red-white-blue. Deep forest green signals trustworthiness and connection to nature/craftsmanship. Warm tan evokes wood grain and Southern hospitality. Burnt orange provides energetic contrast for calls to action without feeling aggressive.

## Typography

| Use      | Family           | Weight Range | Rationale                                              |
| -------- | ---------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Headings | **Bitter**       | 400-700      | Slab serif with strong, dependable character. Readable at large sizes, feels established without being stuffy. |
| Body     | **Source Sans 3** | 300-700      | Clean humanist sans-serif. Excellent readability on screens, professional but approachable. |

Both fonts are available via Google Fonts with no licensing cost.

## Voice & Tone

**Core voice:** Straightforward and honest, like a neighbor who knows what he's doing.

- **Do:** Use plain language. Say "we'll fix it" not "we provide remediation services."
- **Do:** Be warm and direct. Andy is a real person — the copy should sound like him.
- **Do:** Mention specific details (27 years, the Panhandle, storm season).
- **Don't:** Use corporate jargon or overly salesy language.
- **Don't:** Oversell — let the work speak for itself.
- **Don't:** Be vague. Specificity builds trust.

**Example:** "Andy Watts has been fixing homes across the Florida Panhandle for over 27 years. When he shows up, the job gets done right."

## Logo

The primary mark is a text-based SVG wordmark:

- **"AHR"** monogram in white on a rounded primary-green (`#1B4332`) square — used as favicon and compact mark.
- **Full wordmark:** "Affordable Home Repairs" in Bitter Bold, primary-dark color, with "LLC" in Source Sans 3 at a smaller size.
- Minimum clear space: half the height of the monogram square on all sides.
- The logo should never appear on backgrounds with insufficient contrast (minimum 4.5:1 ratio).

## Usage Notes

- Accent color (`#E76F51`) is reserved for primary CTAs (call now, get estimate) and key interactive elements. Do not overuse.
- Section backgrounds alternate between `bg` and `bg-alt` to create visual rhythm without heavy borders.
- All green-on-white text combinations exceed WCAG AA contrast requirements.
